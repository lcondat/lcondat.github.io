About me: I got my PhD in 2006 from Grenoble Inst. of Tech., Grenoble, France. After 2 years as a postdoc in Munich, Germany, I was hired as a permanent researcher by the CNRS in 2008. I spent 4 years in the GREYC, Caen, and 7 years in GIPSA-Lab, Grenoble. Since Nov. 2019, I am on leave from the CNRS and I am a Research Scientist in the Visual Computing Center of KAUST.

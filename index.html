Short bio: I did my PhD in the Lab. of Images and Signals, now part of GIPSA-lab, Grenoble, France. Then I spent 2 years as a postdoc in the Helmholtz Zentrum München, Munich, Germany. Since Nov. 2019, I am on leave from the CNRS and I am a Research Scientist in the Visual Computing Center of KAUST, Thuwal, Saudi Arabia.
